Health Equity Assessment Toolkit (HEAT)

HEAT enables the exploration and comparison of within‑country health inequalities across countries.

To get started, click on Explore Inequality in the blue band at the top and explore the situation within one country of interest. Then go on and click on Compare Inequality to compare the situation in one country with the situation in other countries.

Inequalities can be assessed using disaggregated data and summary measures, that are presented in interactive tables and graphs. Customized results can be exported and saved in different formats. This edition uses data from the WHO Health Equity Monitor database (2016 update).

Suggested citation: Health Equity Assessment Toolkit (HEAT): Software for exploring and comparing health inequalities in countries. Built-in database edition. Version 2.0. Geneva, World Health Organization, 2017.
